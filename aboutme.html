More About Me

Hey this website is pretty cool, how did you make it?

I programmed it myself, every little bit!
From the navagation menu, to the responsive UI, to the cute illustrations and the individual portfolio pages. It took a lot of time and effort, so hopefully it looks good on your device and runs quickly!
No Squarespace or GoDaddy or Wordpress junk here!

What are your favorite hobbies?

My favorite hobbies are biking, hiking and playing video games.
I'm currently reading the book 'Tomorrow and Tomorrow and Tomorrow' and I'm always watching new standup.

Where do you live?

I live in Chicago, IL for the time being, but I spend a lot of time in Colorado too!

What kinda work have you done before?

Everything! While I studied marketing, I've worked as a graphic designer, video editor, consultant, data analyst and a caddie.
I especially enjoy working on the creative side of things, but my programming experience comes in handy too!
