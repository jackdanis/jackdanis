Business School Proposals

These are some conceptual designs I created as part of my classes at the CU Boulder Leeds School of Business

NutriGO Project

This project was a semester long collaborative effort between myself and 4 other students. We had to create a full marketing plan and presentation on how the company would be structured, what our break-even revenue would be and how we would achieve scale.

I created a variety of mockup designs, renderings, and logos for the product while also working heavily on the mathematics to ensure our product would be viable at launch. We also tested the designs with a survey directed towards our target audience and recieved great feedback.
